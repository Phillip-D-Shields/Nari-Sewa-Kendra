Who We Are

Nari Sewa Kendra is Nepali for Womens Service Center, which is exactly what we started as in 1977. Our initial objective was to provide skills training and a support network to women in rural communities. Today our devotion and ambition are much greater, affecting women, children, minorities, and the less fortunate across Nepal.

  • इतिहास History

    We began as Nari Hasta Kala Vikas Kendra in 1977. Shortly after, in 1980 we registered as an official non-profit NGO. Fourteen years later we officially changed our name to Nari Sewa Kendra. Other than a name change and our expanding reach, our core purpose has always been to empower the women in our communities.

  • मिशन Mission

    We are here to bring progressive change to our communities. We have a wide support network that helps women navigate the difficulties of life. We offer skills training and a multitude of educational programs to give them an equal footing in our rapidly changing world. You can view more info here.
